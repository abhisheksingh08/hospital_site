About Us

WE CARE

Raghvendra Hosital offers consultation in all super specialties in Jhansi, with a best-in-class ambience and standards for the convenience of patients. The hospital is spread across 3 floors with fully equipped Diagonistic centre (CT-Scan,MRI,X-ray,Ultrasound) and also Nursing College,offers the best medical care in a facility customized to deliver the highest quality of service.

OUR NETWORK & HEALTH CARE

Raghvendra Hospital is in network with Apollo Hospital,New Delhi and also several hospitals in Jhansi,Gwalior,Kanpur and Lucknow.
Regular Ambulance services available, preserving the critical hospitalization link for offering complete and comprehensive care to patients.
